Over 192,000 Andalusians have to wait an average of 134 days to undergo surgery

Andalusia's recent healthcare waiting lists reveal 841,731 residents await specialist care, with 192,561 awaiting surgery as of June. Specialist care waiting lists decreased by 40,000 in six months, but surgical waiting lists increased by over 20,000. Andalusia leads in patients awaiting specialist care, with an average wait time of 121 days. Dermatology and Traumatology have the highest wait times. Despite having the highest total surgeries pending, Andalusia's average surgical wait time is 139 days. Critics blame mismanagement and privatization, while the government cites improvements since 2018.

The Van Gogh Greatest Hits exhibition returns to Seville with significant technological innovations

"VAN GOGH GRANDES ÉXITOS" exhibition returns to Seville with technological innovations, immersing visitors in virtual canvases, including 3D and UV rooms. The showcase features AI-generated portraits of Van Gogh and offers Instagram users the opportunity to capture photos with an innovative canvas of "Sunflowers."

Seville captivates the world in a memorable Latin Grammy Awards

The 24th edition of the Latin Grammy Awards took place in Seville, filling the Palace of Congresses and Exhibitions (Fibes) with light, color, and, of course, music. A celebration where Shakira took home three gramophones (Song of the Year, Best Pop Song, and Best Urban Fusion). This achievement was shared with Karol G (Album of the Year, Best Urban Music Album, and Best Urban Fusion) and Natalia Lafourcade (Best Singer-Songwriter Album, Best Singer-Songwriter Song, and Record of the Year). A night with a distinctly feminine flamenco accent.

Complaints about delays and breakdowns of trains in Seville: "Will I ever arrive at work on time?"

Seville commuter train breakdown causes one-hour delays on the Seville-Cádiz line at 7:30 am. Normalcy restored by 9:19 am, with passengers transferred to a new Cercanías train. Renfe attributes the disruption to an Osuna-Seville Medium-Distance train breakdown. Commuters express frustration on social media, questioning timely arrivals.

Success of the Science and Culture Week at Tabladilla School

The Science and Culture Week at Tabladilla School stood out for journalism and writing workshops, book-related activities, and educational programs, emphasizing practical and participatory learning. Students explored chemistry and biology labs, took part in the "Solar System" workshop designing planets, and engaged with biodiversity in the Tabladilla School garden.
